About me

My name is Angela Monique Alba and I am a graphic designer with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology degree from the University of Cebu - Main Campus. I have experience using Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, and Canva to create visually appealing designs. In my free time, I enjoy creating digital artworks. My portfolio showcases a selection of my sample works, which demonstrate my design skills and ability to create effective designs. I am passionate about design and always looking to improve my skills. I am a team player and committed to delivering high-quality work on time.

    • Julio Supermart - Logo Design

    The purpose of this work is to design a logo for Julio's Supermart, a grocery store in Cebu City. The objective of the project is to create a modern, professional logo that represents the store's offerings of fresh and preserved food and household items. I began by conducting research on the store's history and values, gathering information from the owner, Mr. Ponce, and looking at other logos in the grocery industry. I then experimented with different design elements, including colors, fonts, and imagery, until I arrived at the final design, which is a blue shopping cart with the store's name inside. Through this project, I learned the importance of thorough research and experimentation in the design process. As a member of the design team at SoftWeb Tech Solutions, my contribution to this project was to create the logo for Julio's Supermart. I worked closely with the owner to ensure that the final design met his vision and provided feedback to the rest of the team throughout the process.

    • Rizal Genealogy - Infographic

    The purpose of this infographic is to provide a visual representation of Rizal's family tree, along with information about their social status in the 19th century. The infographic includes photos of Rizal's parents to provide context and make it more engaging. To create the infographic, I researched Rizal's family and their social status, organized the information in a clear and concise way, and used Canva to create a visual representation. The final product is an informative and visually appealing infographic that displays Rizal's genealogy and the social status of his family. Through this project, I gained knowledge about Rizal's family and their social status, and gained experience in creating infographics and presenting information visually. I also learned from any mistakes I made and used that knowledge to improve the final product.
